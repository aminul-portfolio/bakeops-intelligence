# BakeOps Intelligence — V3 Packaging and Pricing Strategy

## Purpose

This document defines a realistic packaging and pricing strategy for BakeOps Intelligence.

It is a product strategy document only.

It does not mean billing, payments, subscriptions, pricing pages, or commercial customer plans are implemented.

---

## Current Commercial Position

BakeOps Intelligence is currently a commercial-readiness product foundation.

It has:

- trusted analytics pages
- repeatable metric builds
- seeded operational demo data
- gold-layer snapshots
- data quality checks
- BI-ready CSV exports
- commercial positioning documentation
- import readiness documentation
- demo/customer setup documentation
- premium commercial operations dashboard
- beta-readiness documentation
- deployment-readiness checklist

It should not claim to be a fully launched paid SaaS product.

---

## Packaging Principle

Packaging should be based on real implemented value, not imagined future features.

The safest current package description is:

```text
Commercial-ready bakery operations intelligence foundation
```

This means BakeOps can be explained, reviewed, demonstrated, and extended toward commercial usage.

It does not mean the product already supports live subscriptions, payments, automated onboarding, external integrations, or real customer accounts.

---

## First Commercial Audience

The first realistic commercial audience is:

```text
Small bakery owners, bakery managers, or bakery operators who need clearer visibility into product profitability, waste impact, ingredient risk, customer value, data quality, and BI-ready reporting outputs.
```

This audience is likely to value:

- product profitability review
- waste-adjusted margin analysis
- ingredient risk visibility
- customer value reporting
- data quality checks
- BI-ready exports
- simple setup and clear operating workflow

They are less likely to need on day one:

- enterprise multi-tenant SaaS
- complex permissions
- automated billing
- deep integrations
- advanced production scheduling
- full accounting integration

## Pricing Strategy Caution

Pricing should not be finalised before technical scope is implemented.

Current safe position:

```text
Pricing strategy can be explored, but paid billing is not implemented yet.
```

Do not claim:

```text
Paid subscriptions are active.
```

```text
Stripe billing is implemented.
```

```text
Customers can subscribe online.
```

```text
Monthly plans are live.
```

## Possible Pricing Direction

If BakeOps later becomes commercially validated, a realistic pricing direction could be:

| Stage | Pricing Style | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Portfolio/demo | Free review/demo | Best for hiring and product validation |
| Friendly beta | Free or low-cost pilot | Helps collect feedback without overpromising |
| Manual setup pilot | One-off setup fee | Better if onboarding is still manual |
| Early SaaS | Monthly subscription | Only after onboarding/import/deployment are real |
| Advanced integration | Custom pricing | Only if external integrations are genuinely built |

This is strategic thinking only.

No pricing is active in the current codebase.
