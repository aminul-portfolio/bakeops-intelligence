Trust layer

Data quality and reviewer trust

BakeOps V1 should not only display numbers. It should prove that the platform checks whether operational data is complete enough to support trusted metrics, dashboards, and exports.

Required V1 checks

DataQualityIssue generation checklist

These checks should be generated by build_bakery_metrics and visible in Django admin and the dashboard.

Critical

Cake variant without recipe

Without recipe lines, ingredient cost and margin cannot be trusted.

Medium

Ingredient below reorder level

Low stock can affect fulfilment and upcoming production planning.

Medium

Ingredient lot close to expiry

Near-expiry stock should appear in ingredient risk and action cards.

Critical

Paid order without items

Revenue and product performance become unreliable without item records.

Medium

Waste record without reason

Unclassified waste weakens the root-cause analysis for margin leakage.

Medium

Production output not allocated

BatchAllocation should connect production output to customer order items.
