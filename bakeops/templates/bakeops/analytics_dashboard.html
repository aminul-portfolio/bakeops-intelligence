Commercial Operating Workflow

From bakery records to trusted action review.

The dashboard is an operating review path, not a fake live integration claim.

Step 1 Operational records Seeded bakery records model orders, products, customers, ingredients, waste, and occasions.
Step 2 Metric build Repeatable build command creates trusted gold-layer analytics snapshots.
Step 3 Decision pages Pages expose product profit, ingredient risk, waste, customers, occasions, and data quality.
Step 4 Action review Review flags highlight where revenue, margin, waste, or trust issues require attention.
Step 5 BI exports Exported CSV files support reporting, audit, and future business intelligence workflows.
